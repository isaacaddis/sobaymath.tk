About

This math contest is the first of its kind in the South Bay area. Local high school students developed the problems and will run the contest. Middle school math teams associated with the South Bay/Santa Monica MATHCOUNTS Chapter were invited to compete, up to a total limit of 160 students.
Contestants are 6th, 7th, and 8th grade students, supported by schools and coaches. The coordinators and problem creators are prior contest winners associated with local high schools, mostly current and former math club presidents. Here is a table layout diagram.
